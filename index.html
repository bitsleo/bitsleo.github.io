About Us

The word BITAANSH originates from the amalgamation of BIT and aansh; meaning ‘a part of B.I.T.’. For the first time in BIT Sindri, a mega event of this stature is being organised by the collaboration of three clubs, namely Leo Club, Arts club and Painting wing. A week long cultural event, including the star-studded pronite this month of March has numerous secrets to unravel.s

Events

1. Matki-phod: This event is equivalent to its superlative version at the ganesh chaturthi celebrations.
2. Casino royale: An arcade of card games for those who revel at being a poker face.
3. Stroke of genius: Based on the much renowned rangoli contest only in a much modified format.
4. Instant Khichdi: A platform for all those mimicking artists to showcase their talents at copying the famous celebs and actors.
5. Electric heels: If you know how to shake a leg then this platform is the one for you. All you got to do is dance your hearts out.
6. Phoenix: A survival based event meant for the strong-hearted, it tests you on both mental and physical grounds and your ultimate ability of persistence.
7. Hence proved: If you reason your opponents to silence then hop in for this ride to arena of diabolical debate wherein you got to prove your point to proceed further into the event.
8. Tarang: Your voice shall be your identity, your songs your language. This is the stage to prove it to others that your vocals are as mesmerising as any others. Get set to receive an encore from an applauding frenzy mass.
9. Gully cricket and T-surf: Get your strokes correct and ready for the shortest format of the game yet developed.
However if you deter outdoor games, show your prowress in the gaming sensations such as Temple Run and Subway Surfer.
10. Mr. And Mrs BITAANSH : Yes, it all finally waters down to this coveted title. The best of the best shall emerge victorious from the masses as the extravaganza comes to a standstill after a gruelling week of blitzkrieg performances, genius interpretations and graceful advances. To be crowned the best amongst the rest is the zenith of all that could be achieved.
